About Our Factory

In 2010, our story began in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, when our founder started a small workshop dedicated to crafting premium thobes — garments that represent elegance, comfort, and cultural pride.
By 2018, we expanded our vision to India — building a manufacturing base equipped to produce export-grade thobes at scale. This allowed us to blend Saudi expertise with Indian craftsmanship, ensuring superior quality at competitive prices.
Star Angel International is a leading name in the world of premium thobe manufacturing, crafting elegant and authentic thobes inspired by the rich traditions of the Middle East. With a specialization in Saudi, Qatari, Kuwaiti, Bahraini, and Omani (Saba) thobe styles, we bring together cultural heritage and modern craftsmanship to deliver garments that stand apart in quality and prestige.
At Star Angel International, every thobe is stitched under the expertise of highly skilled professional tailors, using finest-grade fabrics, precision cuts, and refined detailing. From classic designs to contemporary finishes, our thobes are engineered for comfort, durability, and a flawless fit that enhances your presence.
Backed by a dedicated team, world-class manufacturing standards, and a passion for excellence, Star Angel International continues to serve clients across the globe with unmatched quality and trustworthy service. We don’t just create thobes — we craft identity, dignity, and distinction.
